About Us

MB Luxury Motors is a student‑run dealership concept created for the AUD (Comp101) Website Design Project. Our mission is to showcase a premium, user‑friendly ecommerce experience inspired by Mercedes‑Benz vehicles. All vehicle names and imagery are used for educational demonstration only—no real transactions occur on this site.

We focus on clarity, simple navigation, and realistic product information: every product includes an image, price, and short description that makes sense for its category. The design language draws from luxury cues—dark backgrounds, silver accents, and clean typography—to highlight the vehicles.
